About My Start-up

Dilan teaching has been active since September 2020 in the UK however currently we are here in Scotland and we have been actively teaching Kurdish and Turkish as in online classroom as well as one-to-one attention. Moreover we have been teaching Kurdish/Turkish history and literature at the same time.

What You will going to learn

We are very delighted to teach you about the History/Culture of Kurdish and Turkish. Furthermore, this is the platform where you can enhance your Grammar, Reading, Writing and Speaking skills for Kurdish/Turkish language. This is going to be hands-on session from scratch to Intermediate.

My Qualification

I have completed my diploma in history from Gazi University. In addition, I have two bachelors degree in 'Sociology' and 'Social Services'. I have done my masters in psychohistory from Aydin Adnan Menderes University.
